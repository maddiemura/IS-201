Global Superstore Tableau

Annual profits are highest in California and New York, with those two states bringing in an annual profit of $76,381 and $74,309 respectively. Annual profit is lowest in Texas and Ohio, which bring in an annual profit of -$25,729 and -$16,971 respectively.























California’s annual profits have increased steadily over the last two years.
























California’s highest profiting product category is office supplies, which brings in $37,748 annually. Technology brings in the second highest profit at $29,470 annual, with the furniture category falling far behind at $9,163 annually.

























California’s profits are forecasted to increase steadily in 2015.
